Welcome to the UploadX Page

What is UploadX?

UploadX is a student project aiming to bring the functionality of the ShareX platform to Linux through Python.

Why?

We enjoy the ease at which ShareX and services like it allow users to upload and distribute images from their computers. We found a lack of such service on Linux distributions and decided to create our own service using Python as a multi-platform distribution method.
